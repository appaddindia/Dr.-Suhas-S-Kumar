Dr. Suhas S Kumar

Suhas S. Kumar, MS (Gen Surg), FIAGES, FALS, Dip Lap, is a distinguished general and laparoscopic surgeon with over 11 years of extensive experience in the field. He is proficient in performing both basic and advanced surgical procedures including complex laparoscopic and laser surgeries. Known for his commitment and precision to patient care, Dr Suhas has handled a wide range of cases earning a reputation for excellence in surgical practice. His expertise spans over various domains of surgery making him a trusted name in the medical community.
